Vision

1.)Equitable Society: Creating an equitable society by collaborating with vulnerable communities.
2.)Inclusivity: Fostering inclusivity and equal opportunities for all members of society.
3.)Empowerment: Empowering individuals and communities to reach their full potential.
4.)Social Impact: Striving for positive and lasting social impact through sustainable initiatives.
5.)Collaboration: Collaborating with diverse stakeholders to address societal challenges.

Mission

1.)Education and Art: Designing and implementing education and art projects to enhance knowledge.
2.)21st-Century Skills: Focusing on developing 21st-century life skills in students through vibrant experiences.
3.)Sustainable Development: Working towards SDG 4, ensuring inclusive and equitable quality education.
4.)Community Development: Contributing to basic education, democracy, lifelong learning.
5.)Livelihood Initiatives: Equipping individuals with digital skills, financial literacy, and vocational training .

Supporting Education:


Our education projects are focused on developing 21st-century life skills in students by providing them with vibrant experiences through research projects or capacity building workshops. Our focus is to work towards Sustainable Development Goal 4 (Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all). It overall contributes to basic education, democracy, facilitating lifelong learning, improving quality of life and promotion of peace.

Creating Livelihood:


We do this through initiatives that equip village youths, women, artisans and other stakeholders with digital skills, financial literacy and marketing techniques, equitable participation, technological support, vocational training, and the implementation of innovative best practices. Capacity development needs a special focus on simultaneously building life skills and vocational skills in the young adults of the hill region. This will safeguard the Himalayan state against excessive migration and uprooting of the social fabric.

TayyariKalKi:


A secure livelihood helps people get out of poverty. While employability has always been a challenge for the under-privileged, the pandemic followed by job losses and massive pay-cuts further pushed them into destitution. The effect of the pandemic on the livelihoods of people belonging to the lesser privileged sections has been profound and long lasting.
